How to Play Wordle

Wordle is a word-guessing game where you have to guess a five-letter word within six attempts. Here's how to play:

  1. Start by guessing a five-letter word.
  2. After each guess, you will receive feedback on your guess:
    • A green square indicates that a letter is in the correct position.
    • A yellow square indicates that a letter is in the word, but in the wrong position.
    • A gray square indicates that a letter is not in the word.
  3. Based on the feedback, make your next guess.
  4. Repeat steps 2 and 3 until you guess the word correctly or run out of attempts.
